Dizziness and Vertigo

I took an Introduction to Vestibular Rehabilitation course and I wanted to make this post to give a background of partly what constitutes dizziness and vertigo for patients, because it can be such a scary and debilitating condition. Also, people don't usually think of Physiotherapists when they get dizziness, so I wanted to shed light on the fact that there are some great clinicians out there who can help assess, treat, and manage dizziness.
