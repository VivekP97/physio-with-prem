About Me

Hi, my name is Prem Parmar! I'm a Physical Therapist who graduated with a Masters in Physical Therapy from the University of Toronto in 2020.

I use a combination of manual therapy, acupuncture/dry needling, cupping, and exercise prescription/education to help my clients achieve their goals. I have a special interest in helping clients with dizziness and post-concussion rehabilitation. My experience working as a personal trainer gives me a unique perspective on how to motivate and progress clients through their goal-oriented exercise programs.

I am extremely passionate about helping my clients return to things that are most important to them. I want to ensure that you have a solid understanding of the underlying drivers for your specific impairments to help develop confidence and independence to ultimately return to doing the things you love

I am a strong believer in life-long learning and continually enroll in post-graduate courses to enhance my skills. I am currently working towards achieving my FCAMPT designationhigher level education in Orthpaedic mobilization/manipulation and have certifications in Acupuncture/Dry needling, and Vestibular/Concussion rehabilitation.
